Pajeon Recipe

Pajeon (파전)

What is Pajeon?

Pajeon is a popular Korean dish, called a Korean pancake. Pajeon usually consists of egg, variety of vegetables, scallions, and sometimes some seafood.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup all purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup potato starch
  • 3/4 tsp sea salt
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 3/4 cup ice water
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped kimchi
  • 4 cups chopped or grated mixed vegetables
  • scallions, cut into 2 inch long sections, thinly sliced
  • 2 tbsp grapeseed or peanut oil

For Sauce

  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tsp rice wine vinegar
  • 1 tsp finely grated ginger or garlic
  • 1/2 tsp sesame oil
  • Pinch of sugar

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, whisk together flour, potato starch, salt, and baking powder.
  2. In another bowl, combine water, egg, and kimchi. Then whisk with the flour mixture until smooth. Fold in vegetables and 3/4 of the scallions./li>
  3. In skillet, heat 2 tbsp oil. Scoot 1/4 cup portions of batter into skillet. Flatten and fry until golden on the bottom. Flip and cook until golden on bottom.
  4. Transfer cooked item to a paper towel lined plate and sprinkle a little more salt. Repeat steps 3 and 4 with however much more batter you want to use.
  5. Make sauce with soy sayce, vinegar, ginger or garlic, sesame oil, and sugar. Sprinkle remaining scallion over pancakes, server with dipping sauce.
